Panel

Large confirmation block at the top of a completion page. Used after a workflow action completes successfully — “Decision sent”, “Article accepted”, “Submission received”. The panel title is the page H1.

Default (success)

Decision sent

Your reference is
DEC-2026-091 
<div class="panel">
  <h1 class="panel-title">Decision sent</h1>
  <div class="panel-body">
    Your reference is<br>
    <strong>DEC-2026-091</strong>
  </div>
</div>

Variants

Compose panel-warning, panel-error, or panel-info.

Action needed

Two reviewer invitations have not been acknowledged.

Something went wrong

The decision could not be saved. Try again in a moment.

Submission received

Your reference is SUB-2026-1247
<div class="panel panel-warning"> … </div>
<div class="panel panel-error"> … </div>
<div class="panel panel-info"> … </div>

Notes

  • The panel title is usually the page H1 — use only one panel per page.
  • The reference number (where present) goes in <strong> on its own line so users can read or quote it back.
  • Pair with back link and follow-up actions below the panel ("Send another decision", "View article").
