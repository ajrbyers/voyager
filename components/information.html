Information

Four patterns for surfacing status messages, in roughly increasing prominence: inset text for ambient context, notice for noteworthy events, warning text for serious or irreversible actions, and error summary for failed form submissions.

Notice — info (default)

Important

Reviewer #419 has not acknowledged the invitation sent on 12 April 2026 — 28 days ago.

<section class="notice" aria-labelledby="info-1">
  <h2 class="notice-head" id="info-1">Important</h2>
  <div class="notice-body">
    <p>Reviewer #419 has not acknowledged the invitation sent on 12 April 2026 — 28 days ago.</p>
  </div>
</section>
{% raw %}{% from "voyager/components/information/information.j2" import notice %}

{% call notice("Important") %}
  <p>Reviewer #419 has not acknowledged the invitation sent on 12 April 2026.</p>
{% endcall %}{% endraw %}

Notice — variants

Compose .notice-warning, .notice-error, or .notice-success alongside .notice.

Action needed

Revision request R-2026-091 is overdue by 4 days.

Something went wrong

The decision could not be saved. Try again or contact support.

Decision sent

Your decision letter was emailed to the corresponding author.

<section class="notice notice-warning"> … </section>
<section class="notice notice-error">   … </section>
<section class="notice notice-success"> … </section>
{% raw %}{% call notice("Action needed", kind="warning") %} … {% endcall %}
{% call notice("Something went wrong", kind="error") %} … {% endcall %}
{% call notice("Decision sent", kind="success") %} … {% endcall %}{% endraw %}

Inset text

Quiet ambient context. No header. Use in the right rail under "Status".

Currently in Under Review. An active minor revision is in flight.

Editorial decision is blocked until at least one further review is received.

<div class="inset-text">
  <p>Currently in <strong>Under Review</strong>. An active minor revision is in flight.</p>
</div>
{% raw %}{% call inset_text() %}
  <p>Currently in <strong>Under Review</strong>. An active minor revision is in flight.</p>
{% endcall %}{% endraw %}

Warning text

For serious advisories — irreversible actions, legal consequences, data loss. Heavier than inset text. The black filled circle is decorative; the visually-hidden “Warning” prefix carries semantics for assistive technology.

! Warning Withdrawing this submission will email the corresponding author and cannot be reversed. 
<div class="warning-text">
  <span class="warning-text-icon" aria-hidden="true">!</span>
  <strong class="warning-text-text">
    <span class="visually-hidden">Warning</span>
    Withdrawing this submission will email the corresponding author and cannot be reversed.
  </strong>
</div>
{% raw %}{% from "voyager/components/warning-text/warning-text.j2" import warning_text %}

{{ warning_text("Withdrawing this submission will email the corresponding author and cannot be reversed.") }}{% endraw %}

Error summary

Used at the top of a form when validation fails. Each error is a link that focuses the corresponding field. role="alert" announces the summary to screen readers immediately.

There is a problem

  • Enter a decision letter to send to the author
  • Choose a decision
<div class="error-summary" role="alert" aria-labelledby="es-1">
  <h2 class="error-summary-title" id="es-1">There is a problem</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><a href="#decision-letter">Enter a decision letter…</a></li>
    <li><a href="#decision-kind">Choose a decision</a></li>
  </ul>
</div>
{% raw %}{% from "voyager/components/information/information.j2"
   import error_summary, error_summary_item %}

{% call error_summary() %}
  {{ error_summary_item("Enter a decision letter to send to the author",
                         anchor="decision-letter") }}
  {{ error_summary_item("Choose a decision", anchor="decision-kind") }}
{% endcall %}{% endraw %}
