Article card

Inbox-style row representing a single article. Carries severity as a coloured left stripe, an ID + tag + days-in-workflow row at the top, the truncated title in the middle, byline at the bottom, and inline action buttons. Alert pills (also documented here) form a quiet footer.

Severity variants

Compose article-card-critical, article-card-warning, or article-card-info. Omit all three for the neutral state.

Alert pills

Small inline status links used at the bottom of an article card to surface alerts. Four variants compose: alert-pill-critical, alert-pill-warning, alert-pill-info, alert-pill-more (for the “+N more” collapse).

Notes

  • Severity is conveyed by both the left stripe colour and the alert pills below, never by stripe alone.
  • The days indicator (article-card-days-critical, -warning) follows the same thresholds as the alert pills: over 60 days critical, over 30 days warning.
  • Truncate titles at the consumer side (use a Django filter or CSS line clamp); the component doesn't enforce a length.
  • Action buttons should use btn-small to keep the row compact.
